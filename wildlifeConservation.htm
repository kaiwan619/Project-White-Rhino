Wild life Conservation

Description

The idea is to collaborate with government agencies/NGOs/Wild life conservationists to use our open source maps to do their own work on them. Like protecting our white rhinos the last of their kind which are still roaming the green pastures of the Kaziranga National park in Assam. Instead of Radio Collars, we put GPS tracking collars on to them to give us live updates of the animal's movement sitting remotely anywhere across the world. The idea could also be implemented as a full conservation project for all animals which are almost on the verge of extinction wherein each animal could be sponsored by an organization or a person and if this could be a proper website or an app on the mobile, then the sponsors would be able to see this as a live feed in their applications/websites and know the whereabouts of their animals. Man has done enough to spoil the balance of nature, it's time we at HERE could #Give back to nature what it has lost by conserving our wild life and by providing a platform to make this a possibility.


This is sample demo page with static data. in real world tool data will be coming from GPS chip. also tool will have option to search by animal and see their live movements as well as see their historical locations which can be filtered according to your search criteria. It can also show heat maps of animals beased on their migration paths.
